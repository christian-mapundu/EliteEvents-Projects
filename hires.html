Hiring Elite Events Catering

Key considerations when choosing our catering services for your event.

1. Menu Quality & Variety

  • Delicious Food: Our primary focus is great-tasting, well-prepared food.
  • Quality Ingredients: We use fresh, high-quality ingredients, sourcing locally in Kariba when possible.
  • Diverse Options: We offer a range of menu choices and styles (buffet, plated, food stations).
  • Tasting Availability: We offer tasting sessions for you to sample our quality and presentation before booking.

2. Flexibility & Customization

  • Dietary Accommodations: We cater to various dietary needs (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergies, religious restrictions).
  • Menu Customization: We work with you to tailor menus to fit your event theme, preferences, and budget.
  • Service Style Options: Choose from different service styles like buffets, family-style, plated dinners, or passed hors d'oeuvres.

3. Professional Service & Staffing

  • Experienced Staff: We provide sufficient, well-trained, professional, and courteous service staff and chefs.
  • On-Site Management: A dedicated manager is present during your event to oversee service.
  • Setup and Cleanup: We clearly define responsibilities for table setting, serving area setup, and post-event cleanup.
  • Appropriate Attire: Our staff are dressed suitably for the formality of your event.

4. Logistics & Equipment

  • Reliable Transport: Especially important in areas like Kariba, we ensure safe and reliable food transport (temperature-controlled).
  • Necessary Equipment: We provide needed items like linens, crockery, cutlery, serving dishes, and glassware.
  • Venue Familiarity: We have experience working in various venues and understand logistical needs.
  • Food Safety Standards: We adhere to strict hygiene and food safety protocols.

5. Clear Pricing & Contracts

  • Transparent Pricing: You receive detailed, itemized quotes with no hidden fees.
  • Clear Contract: We provide a comprehensive contract outlining all agreed services, menus, timelines, costs, and policies.

6. Reputation & Reliability

  • Positive Reviews & References: We have excellent testimonials and references available.
  • Experience: We have a proven track record catering diverse events.
  • Licenses & Insurance: We hold proper business licenses, food handling certifications, and adequate liability insurance.
  • Responsiveness: We pride ourselves on clear communication throughout the planning process.

7. Beverage Services & Essentials

  • Non-Alcoholic Options: We offer a range of suitable non-alcoholic beverage packages and options.
  • Glassware Provision: We supply appropriate and clean glassware for all beverages served.
